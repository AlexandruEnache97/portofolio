The Cube in the Dark

    Is my first game released on Google Play Store.

    The Cube in the Dark is developed in Unity. The goal of the game is to control a cube, avoid bombs and teleport between rooms to collect the key of the level and escape the room by reaching the door.

    The controls of the game are a joystick which controls the movement of the cube and a button which allows the cube to jump and climb the walls of the room.

    The game has 15 levels, every level store the time needed to pass the level in order to challenge the player to beat his score.

    The concept is very simple. In addition to all of this, every level has a number of coins to collect. With the coins, the player can buy different skins for their cube.

    The link in the Google Play Store:

    https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.RaikamGames.TheCubeintheDark
